Experience the Difference in Care

Sree Manju Hospital is one of the best hospitals in Hyderabad and was established in 2021 by Dr. Praveen Puralasetty, renowned Radiologist in Hyderabad. Constructed in the 2020, the Center is equipped with the most-up-to-date seismic-safety features and high-tech medical apparatus as well as a sophisticated and energy-efficient climate control system designed to maximize the comfort of doctors, patients and staff.

Dedicated to offering health care services that are welcoming, accessible, safe and respectful for everyone, Sree Manju Hospitals has engaged patients, families and community representatives and leaders as active partners to ensure that their voices are heard and included in every area of its operations.

The cornerstones of Sree Manju Hospitals are its unstinting focus on clinical excellence, affordable costs, modern technology and patient friendly environment. Sree Manju Hospitals was among the first few hospitals in the city to leverage technology to facilitate seamless healthcare delivery. The organization embraced the rapid advancement in health industry.
